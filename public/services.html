Vaman

Vaman is a purification therapy that expels vitiated Kapha and Pitta doshas from the body through therapeutic vomiting. Uses= Effective for chronic colds, coughs, asthma, shortness of breath, skin diseases, psoriasis, vitiligo, diabetes, thyroid disorders, obesity, hyperacidity, indigestion, tonsillitis, lymphatic swelling, recurrent fever, and various Kapha and Pitta-related diseases

Virechan

Virechan is a purification therapy that expels vitiated Pitta and Kapha doshas from the body through therapeutic purgation. Uses= Beneficial for jaundice, liver diseases, spleen diseases, gallstones, constipation, hyperacidity, hemorrhoids, fistula, piles, various skin diseases (scabies, eczema), diabetes, obesity, worm infestations, urinary disorders, gout, burning sensation in the body, headache, burning in the eyes, chronic fever, herpes, and various Pitta and Kapha-related diseases

Basti

Basti is a purification therapy where medicated oils or decoctions are administered through the anal route, primarily balancing Vata dosha. Uses= Highly beneficial for chronic constipation, bloating, abdominal pain, joint pain, waist pain, knee pain, sciatica, paralysis, tremors, muscle weakness, infertility, sexual debility, menstrual disorders, PCOS, neurological disorders, Parkinson's disease, and various Vata-related diseases.

Nasya

asya involves administering medicated oils or powders through the nostrils, primarily for diseases above the clavicle (neck and head region). Uses = Effective for chronic sinusitis, headache, migraine, hair fall, premature graying of hair, ear pain, eye diseases, dental diseases, paralysis of the face, insomnia, stress, memory loss, and various diseases of the head and neck.

Swedan (Fomentation/Sweating):

Swedan involves various methods of inducing sweating to open up channels and remove toxins. Uses= Effective for body aches, stiffness, joint pain, back pain, sciatica, frozen shoulder, muscle cramps, paralysis, swelling, colds, coughs, and various Vata and Kapha-related diseases.

Raktamokshan

Raktamokshan is a purification therapy involving controlled bloodletting to remove vitiated blood. Uses= Beneficial for various skin diseases (scabies, eczema, ringworm), chronic wounds, boils, carbuncles, abscesses, vitiligo, psoriasis, gout, arthritis, allergic reactions, and various blood-related disorders.

Abhyanga (Oil Massage)
