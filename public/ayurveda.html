What is Ayurveda?

Ayurveda is India’s ancient healing system that balances body, mind, and spirit through personalized therapies and natural practices.

It focuses on prevention, balance, and long-term wellness.

The Three Doshas

Vata

Controls movement and creativity. Balanced Vata brings energy and clarity.

Pitta

Representing fire and water, Pitta governs digestion and metabolism. Excess may cause anger or inflammation.
Balance with cooling foods, mint, and calming practices.

Kapha

Made of earth and water, Kapha provides strength and immunity.
Imbalance can cause lethargy and weight gain.Balance with spices, exercise, and stimulation.

Key Ayurvedic Practices

Ayurveda emphasizes daily and seasonal routines (Dinacharya and Ritucharya) including oil massage, meditation, yoga, and sattvic foods.

Panchakarma therapies cleanse the body through detoxification techniques, awakening the body’s innate healing power based on the five elements.
